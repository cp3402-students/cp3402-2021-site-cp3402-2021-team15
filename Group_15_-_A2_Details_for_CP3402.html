Local Environments

The local development environment we used was XAMPP as it was the easiest platform for all uses to setup effieciently.
Our group initially tried many different environments but few of us were able to get them running on our local machines correctly.
By using XAMPP the setup was simple and direct to get our database and wordpress server up and running.
